A perfectly prepared meal, a chocolate cake artistically packaged and presented…. That is what we do…impeccable taste with a dash of modern simplicity.Pastry Emporium is the latest evolution of years of New York restaurant experience steeped in authentic European roots. Born as a bakery called My Most Favorite Dessert, then moved to Madison Avenue and then to Midtown while its name also continued changing. The fully renovated Pastry Emporium Bakery now makes its home on the Upper West Side as a chic bakery.
